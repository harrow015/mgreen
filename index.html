WASTE MNAGEMENT

The activities and actions necessary to manage garbage from its inception to its final disposal are referred to as waste management. Waste management includes the treatment of both solid and liquid waste. It also provides numerous options for recycling goods that are not classified as garbage during the process.

About recycling

Recycling is the process of converting waste materials into new materials and objects. The recovery of energy from waste materials is often included in this concept. The recyclability of a material depends on its ability to reacquire the properties it had in its original state.
